Salmon Bagel

Starting with smoked salmon, this bagel sandwich is layered with tasty ingredientsâ€”flavored cream cheese, capers, and purple onion. It's a quick fix for a brown-bag lunch.

ingredients

  • 1 pumpernickel or plain bagel (6 oz.)
  • 2 tablespoons chive cream cheese
  • 1/3 cup thinly sliced English cucumber
  • 3 ounces thin-sliced smoked salmon
  • 3 thin rings red onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon drained capers

Steps

  1. Split bagel in half horizontally. Spread cut sides with cream cheese on 1 bagel half.
  2. Layer cucumber, smoked salmon, red onion, and capers.
  3. Set remaining bagel half, cream cheese side down, over filling.
